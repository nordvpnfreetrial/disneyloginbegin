How to Watch Disney+ on Your Device


Watch Disney+ on your Smart TV or streaming device by entering the on-screen code at disneyplus.com/begin and logging into your account. Start enjoying your favorite shows and movies instantly!

Step-by-Step: How to Watch disneyplus.com/begin/start on Your Device


Follow these exact steps to activate Disney+ and start streaming on your Smart TV, streaming stick, game console, or other supported device:

  1. Power on your Smart TV or streaming device.
  2. Ensure your device is connected to the internet.
  3. Install the Disney+ app from your device’s app store (if not already installed).
  4. Launch the Disney+ app.
  5. You will see a screen with an 8-digit activation code — leave this on your screen.
  6. On a separate device (phone, tablet, or computer), open a browser.
  7. Visit disneyplus.com/begin.
  8. Enter the 8-digit code exactly as shown on your TV/device.
  9. Log in using your Disney+ account credentials.
  10. After successful login, your device will activate automatically and you can start watching!
